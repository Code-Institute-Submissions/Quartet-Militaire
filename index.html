About Us

Quartet Militaire is a string quartet based in London, UK. We pride ourselves in playing music tailored for your special occasion (e.g. weddings and formal dinners). Our repertoire ranges from classical pieces like Handel’s Hornpipe to pop pieces from the likes of Ed Sheeran to the Beatles. We also accommodate any musical requests.

Looking for a friendly, professional string quartet? Contact us today for a personalised quote.

Weddings

Quartet Militaire have years of experience providing music for weddings. We are happy to work closely with you to form a tailor-made package that is going to make your big day one to remember! Prices start from £500 including VAT and travel costs. We love to travel and are more than happy to come to your chosen location. Please bear in mind that additional travel costs will be reflected in your personalised quotation.

Ready to make your dream wedding day a reality? Get in touch, and we can bring the joy of music to your happy day.
